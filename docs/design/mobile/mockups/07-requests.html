07 — Requests

The Requests tab is the human's triage surface: role-grouped ("Needs your answer" / "Waiting on others" / "Answered — act on it" / "Done"), one detail screen, and per-role action sheets (respond, accept/reject task asks, close, convert to task). Spec: flows/07-requests.md. All endpoints exist today; grouping is client-side over the container's request list, scoped to the paired human's agent id.
